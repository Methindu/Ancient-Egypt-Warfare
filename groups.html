Groups Fighting in the Battle of Kadesh

The battle of kadesh included the Hitties and Egyptians, here are the details, note that all facts were from the same website so they wont be listed multiple times

Egyptians

Ruler: Pharaoh Rameses II

Infantry: 20,000 soldiers (Kerrigan M, n.d, https://www.britannica.com/event/Battle-of-Kadesh)

Chariots: 2,000 chariots

Objective: Expand teritory

Hitties
